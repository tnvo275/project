A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

My name is Tran and I come from Vietnam. I am currently studying at University of Houston ( link ) in the College of Technology (link ). Even though it seems to be impossible, one of my goals is to travel through every states in the U.S. before my graduation in 2019. I have been in the U.S for 2.5 years and I have only visited 5 states now but I am still working on reaching that goal. Let me tell you about my journey.

  1. Places that I visited

    Time States Cities Duration
    Fall 2015 Texas Austin 1 day
    Winter 2015 Washington, D.C. 3 days
    Spring 2016 California Los Angeles 2 days
    Arizona Phoenix 4 days
    Winter 2017 California San Francisco 5 days
    Washington Seattle 5 days

    Among these, I am most impressed by the beauty of the capital city, Washington D.C. That was also my first out-of-state trip. The city is very different from a Southern state like Texas. It was so beautiful that it captivated me right away at the very first sight. I really love the old architecture buildings; they gave me the feeling that I was in Europe. Moreover, there are lots of museums resided inside the National Mall which makes the city even more interesting. I also got to try the underground transportation in U.S. for the first time and it was fun

  2. My most recent trip

    Last winter I went on a trip to San Francisco, California for 5 days and Seattle for another 5 more days. It was a pleasant trip since I got to spend it with my best friend. San Francisco, which is called the city of hills, is a windy, yet beautiful place. I went there on winter so it was undoubtedly cold. The city which was once hippie heartland in the past still had that spirit of freedom, love, and peace everywhere from the colorful rainbow flags hanged along a whole street to carefree graffiti images embedded on every corner of the city. I made it to some of the city’s most famous destination such as the Golden Gate Bridge, the Fisherman’s Wharf as well as the Lombard Street. I also tried riding on San Francisco’s unique cable car up and down numerous hills and ate delicious food. It was such an unforgettable trip to me. Below are some pictures I took during the trip.
